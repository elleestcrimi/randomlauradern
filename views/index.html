About Laura Dern

Laura Dern is an award-winning actress known for her roles in such films as Jurassic Park & Star Wars. She’s also a well-known feminist working hard to level the playing field for minorities in many industries — including tech.

She recently participated in a Young Women Code event sponsored by Booking.com. To celebrate her participation, two of the code mentors made this Random Photo Generator for the code students to add placeholder images to their sites.

This site was created with smiles & high-fives by Hadeer Younis & Erin Weigel.
